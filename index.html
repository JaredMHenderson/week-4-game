Crystal Collecter Game

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game

There are four cystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of the crystal
